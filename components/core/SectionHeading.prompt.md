Opens every major section. Eyebrow is a small flame-colored uppercase label (optionally prefixed with a mono index like "01"); title is the heavy Archivo display line underneath.

```jsx
<SectionHeading index="02" eyebrow="Selected Work" title="Things I've made" />
```

Keep titles short — 2-5 words. Don't center unless the section is a standalone full-bleed moment (e.g. a closing CTA).
