Small pill for a skill, tool, tag or year — mono type, hairline border, no fill.

```jsx
<Tag>Premiere Pro</Tag>
<Tag variant="accent">2026</Tag>
```

Variants: `default` (muted border, secondary text) for most tags; `accent` (flame border + text) to call out one item in a list (e.g. current role, featured skill). Don't mix more than one `accent` tag per row — it's a highlight, not a category.
